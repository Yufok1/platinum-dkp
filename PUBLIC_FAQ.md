# Public FAQ

## What is Platinum DKP?

Platinum DKP is an off-chain contribution game for a meme coin community.

You do quests. You keep receipts. Reviewers award DKP for useful work.

## Is DKP money?

No. DKP is reputation, status, and contribution tracking. It is not a token, dividend, payout claim, or promise of return.

## Do I need to buy tokens to earn DKP?

No. Buying, holding, selling, or trading volume cannot be required for DKP.

## What can DKP unlock?

DKP can unlock community roles, lore ranks, cosmetic status, public recognition, reviewer eligibility, or proposal eligibility.

It does not automatically unlock treasury, token allocations, or payments.

## What is a quest?

A quest is a scored contribution with a clear objective and proof requirement.

Example:

```text
Help a newcomer understand the rules.
Submit a link to the answer.
Reviewer checks it.
Receipt gets recorded.
```

## What are dragons?

Dragons are problem classes.

- FUD Dragon: confusion
- Scam Dragon: fake links and impersonators
- Lore Dragon: memes and explanations
- Onboarding Dragon: beginner help
- Archive Dragon: receipts and records
- Chaos Dragon: drama
- Whale Aggro Dragon: private entitlement trying to become policy

## Can the GPT move treasury?

No. The GPT can draft proposals and check rules. It cannot approve or execute treasury movement.

## Is this financial advice?

No. Do not treat DKP, memes, quests, jokes, or community posts as financial advice.
