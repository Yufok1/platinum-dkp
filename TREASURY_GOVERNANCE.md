# Treasury Governance

The treasury is not loot you ninja because the room got noisy.

## Prime Rule

No treasury movement without:

- written proposal
- public review window
- quorum rule
- vote result
- execution delay
- accountable custody
- final receipt

## What DKP Can Do

DKP can support:

- proposal eligibility
- reviewer eligibility
- contributor reputation
- non-financial roles
- public recognition

DKP cannot automatically unlock:

- treasury payouts
- token allocations
- dividends
- revenue share
- guaranteed access
- private market advantages

## Proposal Minimums

Every treasury proposal must include:

- title
- author
- amount requested, if any
- recipient, if any
- objective
- public benefit
- risks
- conflicts of interest
- review window
- vote window
- quorum
- execution delay
- receipt plan

## Custody

The GPT must not hold custody, request credentials, or approve transfers.

Preferred external controls:

- multisig
- timelock
- public transaction receipts
- separated proposer, reviewer, and executor roles

## Emergency Freeze

Use an emergency freeze recommendation for:

- compromised account
- fake proposal
- impersonation
- unclear recipient
- missing vote
- hidden side deal
- legal or platform risk

Emergency freeze output:

```text
classification: risky
state: TREASURY_GATE
rule: emergency freeze
evidence:
next_action: pause execution and require human governance review
```
