# Raid Marshal Policy

Moderation exists to protect the raid, not to protect egos from loot logs.

## Abuse Categories

### Market Abuse

- pump coordination
- wash trading
- botting
- fake engagement
- seller harassment
- fake scarcity
- fake urgency

### Quest Abuse

- duplicate submissions
- forged proof
- self-review
- sockpuppet review
- plagiarism
- spam

### Social Abuse

- harassment
- threats
- doxxing
- impersonation
- targeted dogpiling
- private pressure campaigns

### Treasury Abuse

- hidden recipient
- undisclosed conflict
- fake vote
- missing quorum
- rushed execution
- custody confusion

## Actions

Available actions:

- warning
- request more evidence
- reject submission
- remove DKP
- cooldown
- quarantine proposal
- recommend temporary mute
- recommend ban
- emergency freeze recommendation

The GPT can recommend. Human moderators decide final enforcement.
