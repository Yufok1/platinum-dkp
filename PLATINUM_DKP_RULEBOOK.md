# Platinum DKP Rulebook

Platinum DKP is an off-chain contribution game. It is not an investment product, profit-sharing plan, trading group, or treasury entitlement system.

## Rule 1: The Token Is The Banner

The token can represent the community culture. It does not make price the game.

No rule may require buying, holding, selling, trading volume, or wallet balance as the basis for DKP.

## Rule 2: DKP Is Reputation

DKP tracks contribution, reliability, receipts, and community service.

DKP can grant:

- roles
- titles
- lore status
- cosmetic ranks
- public credit
- proposal eligibility
- reviewer eligibility

DKP cannot automatically grant:

- treasury payouts
- token allocations
- dividends
- guaranteed access
- market advantages
- private deal flow

## Rule 3: Quests Must Have Proof

A quest without proof is vibes with a hat. Nice hat. Still not scored.

Every scored quest needs:

- objective
- proof required
- DKP range
- cooldown
- abuse cases
- reviewer
- receipt

## Rule 4: Treasury Movement Needs Governance

No pot movement without:

- written proposal
- public review
- quorum
- vote
- execution delay
- accountable custody
- final receipt

No single private actor gets to become loot council, raid leader, bank alt, and auction house at the same time.

## Rule 5: Market Manipulation Is Not Gameplay

Rejected:

- pump planning
- buy/sell/hold pressure
- price predictions
- coordinated posting to move price
- seller harassment
- whale-only commands
- fake urgency
- fake scarcity
- wash trading
- botting
- fake engagement

Allowed:

- neutral education
- lore
- public risk notes
- scam warnings
- onboarding
- contribution quests
- governance proposals

## Rule 6: Humor Is Not A Loophole

Dry humor is allowed. Burying risk in jokes is not.

Every serious rule must remain readable without knowing the joke.

## Rule 7: Receipts Beat Drama

If there is a dispute, produce:

- original rule
- original quest
- proof submitted
- decision
- appeal evidence
- final outcome

If there is no receipt, classify as `not yet supported`.

## Rule 8: Humans Hold Final Authority

The GPT can draft, classify, warn, and recommend. It cannot be final authority over funds, identity, law, custody, or permanent bans.

## Rule 9: Patch Notes Are Mandatory

Rule changes need visible patch notes:

- what changed
- why it changed
- when it starts
- whether it applies retroactively
- how old quests are handled

## Rule 10: Newcomers Get A Fair Door

The system should be legible to people who do not know old MMO slang, crypto slang, or community lore.

Use plain text first. Add jokes second. The raid can have flavor after the map exists.
